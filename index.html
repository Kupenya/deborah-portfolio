Who I Am

I'm a Social Media Manager with 2+ years of experience helping brands connect authentically with their audiences while keeping a consistent brand voice.

I combine strategy with creative work, using data to guide decisions. What I do differently is handle everything from start to finish: I plan campaigns, create content, edit videos, write copy, and analyze results. As a full-service social media manager and content creator, I can take care of all your social media needs.

Think of me as the translator who makes sure your audience understands what you're trying to say.
